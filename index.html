Our Sacred Services

Experience divine rituals and ceremonies with us.

Vivah Sanskar & Rituals

From **Kundali Milan** and **Pili Chitti** to **Lagana Patrika** and **Vivah Sampann Karana**, we ensure every marital ritual is performed meticulously and traditionally, including guidance on **Shubh Vivah Tithi**.

Pujas & Anushthans

Experience profound blessings with **Satyanarayan Katha & Havan**, **Rudrabhishek**, **Grah Shanti Puja**, **Sunderkand Path**, and **Murti Sthapana**. All rituals are conducted as per Vedic traditions.

Special & Ancestral Rites

We organize **Samuhik Durga Puja**, **Navratri Puja & Saptashati Path** at your home, and arrange **Jagran Mandali**.

Shradh Karma

We also provide complete arrangements for Shradh Karma, including Sajja Daan Samagri Ke sath, Pandit, and Mahapatra services.
